What we offer

Fitness 365 is 5,500 sq ft gym based in Limerick City, alongside access to a world class training factility, We offer 1 to 1 coaching, customized nutrition plans to suit your needs and we also have group classes daily. Whatever your training goals are we can help you accomplish them.
